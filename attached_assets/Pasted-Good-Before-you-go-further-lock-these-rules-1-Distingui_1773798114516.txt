Good. Before you go further, lock these rules:

1. Distinguish 3 separate concepts:
- schedule phase tags = expansion / structuring / recovery
- nutrition regime ids = base, carbup, carbcut, fatcut, recomp, deload, dietbreak, peakbulk
- orbital physiology phases = priming, loading, accumulation, saturation, partitioning, resensitization, rebound, expression

These are NOT the same field.

2. Do not collapse my nutrition regime ids directly into orbital physiology phases.
Instead use a translator table with:
- nutrition_phase_id
- orbital_phase_primary
- orbital_phase_secondary

3. Also do not treat meal_template_id as the same thing as nutrition_phase_id.
meal_template_id should parse into:
- nutrition_phase_id
- meal_slot

Example:
base_precardio =>
- nutrition_phase_id = base
- meal_slot = precardio

- carbcut -> partitioning | resensitization
- fatcut -> partitioning | resensitization
- recomp -> partitioning | expression
- deload -> resensitization | priming
- dietbreak -> rebound | expression

7. Keep subsystem phases separate in the app:
- daily_schedule_mode = expansion / structuring / recovery

- orbital_phase = physiology interpretation layer
- later we may also add sleep_phase and training_phase

8. Do not assume better adherence always means a better schedule.
A mismatch may reflect sensory overload, wrong block timing, wrong food timing, or wrong state-fit.
